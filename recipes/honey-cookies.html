Grandma Honey Cookies

Description

Soft and sweet Honey Cookies made in one bowl with basic pantry ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter – 100g

  • Eggs – 1 Entire Egg + 1 Yoke

  • Sugar – 4 Flat tbsp

  • Honey – 3 tbsp

  • Baking Soda – 1/2 tsp

  • Cornflour – 1/2 Cup

  • Flour – 1.5 Cup

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients

  2. Let Rest 20 min in fridge

  3. Divide dough in small chunks on a baking tray

  4. Bake for 17 min on 180C

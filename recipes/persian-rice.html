Persian Rice

Description

There is something truly magical that happens when sliced potatoes are friend along with the rice at the bottom of the pot!

Ingredients

  • Potato – 1.

  • Cooked Rice – 2 Cups.

  • Saffron – 1 tbsp Brewed in Hot Water.

  • Canola Oil

  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Slice potatoes as evenly as possible in about 1/4″ rounds.

  2. Gather all of the needed ingredients.

  3. Fry the potato slices – Add oil to a pot, tbsp of safron, and a sprinkle of salt. Stir with a wooden spatula and add the potatos, sprinkle salt on each slice.

  4. Add rice – Cook on high for 10 min, then lower the temp, wrap cover in a towel and steam for about one hour
